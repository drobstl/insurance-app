# Empty-state Loom scripts

Short walkthrough videos for the feature empty states + Resources → Video
Tutorials. Each is ~30–45 seconds — read it straight through, conversational,
like you're showing a fellow agent over their shoulder. Record in Loom, copy the
share link, and it drops into the matching slot.

---

## Referrals
**Slot:** Referrals page empty state · env `NEXT_PUBLIC_REFERRALS_WALKTHROUGH_LOOM_URL`

> Here's the part most agents sleep on. Every client who downloads your app has a
> "Refer a Friend" button. They tap it, pick a contact, and send your card with a
> personal intro. From there, the AI texts that referral from a number that sounds
> like you — real iMessage, blue bubbles, ninety-nine percent read rate — and it
> actually has a conversation. It warms them up, handles the basic questions, and
> drops a booking link when they're ready. You just watch it happen right here, and
> jump in any time you want. This is how you turn one closed deal into three more,
> without chasing anybody.

## Rewrites
**Slot:** Rewrites page empty state · env `NEXT_PUBLIC_REWRITES_WALKTHROUGH_LOOM_URL`

> Your back book is sitting on money, and this is how you go get it. Every policy
> you write has a one-year anniversary. AFL watches all of them, and the moment one
> hits, the AI texts that client to revisit their rate — see if there's something
> better now that their situation's changed. You don't lift a finger. The campaigns
> show up on this page, you see who's responding, and you step in to close. Old
> business becomes new business, automatically — while everybody else forgets their
> clients exist the day after the sale.

## Retention
**Slot:** Retention page empty state · env `NEXT_PUBLIC_RETENTION_WALKTHROUGH_LOOM_URL`

> Losing a policy to a lapse is the worst money you'll ever leave on the table —
> you already earned it. So here's your safety net. The second a policy's at risk,
> you flag it from the client's page, or you just forward the carrier's lapse notice
> to your AFL email — and we catch it. AFL pulls the right carrier number, runs the
> save outreach, and tracks every step right here so nothing slips. You see what's
> at risk, what's been saved, and what still needs you. This is how you keep the
> business you fought to win.
